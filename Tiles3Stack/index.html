🎮 Game Features

🔄

Immersive Stacking & Matching Mechanics

In Tiles 3 Stack, gameplay revolves around stacking colorful tiles. Your goal is to connect three or more tiles of the same type horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to clear them and score big points!

🚀

Multi-level Challenges

Embark on a challenging journey where each level offers unique tile arrangements and stacking requirements. Difficulty gradually increases, allowing your skills to grow continuously!

💫

Special Clearing Wand

Use the powerful clearing wand tool to remove any tile with a single tap, helping you overcome difficult situations and achieve higher scores. Use it wisely as it's a limited resource!
