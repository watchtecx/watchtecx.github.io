How to Play

Simple & Addictive Gameplay

  1. 1

    Find Matching Stars: Look for groups of adjacent stars with the same color.

  2. 2

    Tap to Clear: Simply tap on a group to clear them from the board.

  3. 3

    Score Points: The larger the group you clear, the more points you'll earn!

  4. 4

    Strategic Clearing: Plan your moves to create larger groups for maximum points.

  5. 5

    Clear the Board: Try to clear the entire board for bonus points and satisfaction!
