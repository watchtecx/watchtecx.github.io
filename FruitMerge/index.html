How to Play on Your Wrist

Master the art of fruit merging with these simple controls optimized for your iWatch:

1

Swipe & Merge

Tap and swipe to move fruits left or right as they drop into the container. When two matching fruits meet, they merge into a larger, higher-scoring fruit. Chain merges to unlock bigger rewards!

2

Precision Placement

Every inch of space matters. Plan each move to set up combos and prevent the fruit stack from spilling over the edges. Timing and strategy are key!

3

Survive the Stack

As fruits grow, the container fills up fast. Keep your cool and manage space wisely to avoid game over. How many watermelons can you create before the pressure builds?
