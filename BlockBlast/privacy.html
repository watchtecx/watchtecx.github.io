Welcome to Block Jam Blaster ("the App"), developed by WatchTec ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information collected, used, or shared when you use the App. Please read this Policy carefully to understand our policies and practices.

2. No User Data Collection

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or user information through the App. This includes but is not limited to:

The App is designed to operate without requiring any personal information from you. You can use the App completely anonymously.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. We do not share any information with third parties for any purpose, as there is no user data to share.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.
